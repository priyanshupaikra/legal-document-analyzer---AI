About ClauseIQ

Learn more about our mission to simplify legal documents with AI technology.

Our Mission

At ClauseIQ, we believe that understanding legal documents shouldn't require a law degree. Our mission is to democratize legal understanding by making complex legal language accessible to everyone through the power of artificial intelligence.

What We Do

ClauseIQ is an AI-powered legal document analyzer that helps individuals and businesses understand the complex terms and potential risks in their legal documents. Whether you're a law student, startup founder, or business professional, our platform provides plain-language explanations of legal clauses, highlights potential risks, and offers actionable suggestions.

Clause Extraction

Automatically identify and extract key clauses from complex legal documents.

Plain Language

Translate complex legal terms into simple, understandable language.

Risk Flagging

Identify potential risks and problematic clauses that need attention.

How It Works

Our platform uses advanced natural language processing and machine learning models to analyze legal documents. We leverage Google's Gemini AI to understand context, extract meaning, and provide actionable insights. The system is continuously learning and improving to better serve our users.

Who We Serve

ClauseIQ is designed for a wide range of users:

  • Law Students: Quickly understand complex legal documents and case materials
  • Startup Founders: Review contracts and agreements without expensive legal fees
  • Small Businesses: Understand the terms they're agreeing to in vendor contracts
  • Freelancers: Review client agreements and protect their interests
  • HR Professionals: Analyze employment contracts and policies
  • Anyone: Who needs to understand a legal document but doesn't have a law degree
